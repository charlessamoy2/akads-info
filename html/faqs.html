Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I start using AKADS already?
    Unfortunately, you cannot use AKADS yet as it still hasn't officially launched. The links that allows you to sign up as a Tutor or Tutee will add you to the waiting list. Once AKADS launches, you'll be the first to know!
  • Can I book multiple sessions in one transaction?
    Yes, you can! There's no need to do the same steps again and again.
  • How does AKADS work?
    The tutee will first have to request for tutoring sessions based on the subject, date and time, and location. Then, just like the ride-sharing app Grab, AKADS will send this request to many competent tutors that teach the subject and can tutor in the specfied location. Once a tutor accepts, just wait for the scheduled tutoring days and you're good to go!
  • How long can a booking last?
    The tutee can book for as long as 3 months straight with the same tutor.
  • Can you rebook the same tutor after a booking session?
    As of this version, you cannot. This is something we are taking into consideration and will definitely add as a feature in the near future.
